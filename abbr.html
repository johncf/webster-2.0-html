Abbreviations

Numerous abbreviations, sometimes not intuitive, appear in Webster's Dictionary 1913; this page catalogs them for reference.

  • 2d = second
  • 3d = third
  • cf. = confer

  • contr. = contracted, contraction

  • obs. = obsolete
  • R. = rare
